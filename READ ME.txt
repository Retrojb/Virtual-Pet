Well guess what its back!

HOW TO PLAY

Enter a name for your pet.

You have 20 moves to play with your pet.

you can feed it
	adds 10 to hunger
	subtracts 10 from rest
you can give you pet a drink
	adds 10 to thirst
	subtracts 10 from play
you can have pet rest
	adds 10 to rest
	subtracts 10 from hunger
you can play with your pet
	add 10 to play
	subtracts 10 from thirst

over 20 turns you can do as you please.
unable to get the pet to "pass away" if status reach 0.
